Baked Chicken Wings Recipe

These baked chicken wings are super crispy and flavorful. They're perfect for game day, parties, or just a fun dinner at home. You can customize the seasoning to your liking, and they're so easy to make!


How to Bake Chicken Wings

You'll find the full step-by-step instructions below, but here's what you can expect from this baked chicken wings recipe:

Chicken Wing Ingredients

These are the simple ingredients you’ll need to make this baked chicken wing recipe:

  • Oil: Olive oil locks in the moisture and gives the seasonings something to stick to.
  • Seasonings: You can use a store-bought seasoning blend or make your own with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika.
  • Chicken Wings: You can use fresh or frozen chicken wings for this recipe.

How to Bake Chicken Wings In the Oven

Here's a brief overview of what you can expect when you bake chicken wings at home:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Season the chicken wings with oil and seasonings.

  3. Place the wings on a baking sheet and bake for 45-50 minutes.

  4. Flip the wings halfway through the cooking time.

  5. Broil the wings for a few minutes to make them extra crispy.

  6. Let the wings rest for a few minutes before serving.

How to Serve Baked Chicken Wings

These baked chicken wings are delicious on their own, but you can also serve them with your favorite dipping sauce. Here are a few ideas:

  • Buffalo Sauce: Toss the wings in buffalo sauce for a classic flavor.
  • BBQ Sauce: Brush the wings with BBQ sauce for a sweet and tangy twist.
  • Ranch Dressing: Serve the wings with ranch dressing for a cool and creamy dip.
